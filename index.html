Welcome To Wagga Canvas

With a staff of enthusiastic and passionate craftsmen operating out of our wagga wagga location, we guarantee a job of the highest quality that is done right first time.

Business owner, Benny started work in the “climate proof canvas” field in 2009 in Melbourne. This factory in Melbourne focused on a fast turnaround, whilst still producing competitively priced work at a high quality. This led to the business growing quickly and becoming the largest in the area. Working in this company he has learned the quality work, customer satisfaction and use of many tools and machinery in a professional manner.

In 2013 Benny made the decision to change the job and start in “On the move Caravans” as a Upholster Since this job, Benny has ensured that the reputation of a quality Upholster has grown to one of reliability and complete customer satisfaction using the principles he has used – and still uses – in his “Wagga canvas and upholstery” business.

With approximately 8 years’ experience, Benny guarantees that whether you are working on your car, truck, boat, trimming banner and sewing or a canvas project that you are more than pleased with the end result.
